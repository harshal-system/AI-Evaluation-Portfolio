# Case Study 01 – Evaluating an AI Explanation of Reaction Rate

## Domain

Chemical Engineering

---

## Objective

Evaluate the quality of an AI-generated explanation about why increasing temperature increases the rate of a chemical reaction.

---

# User Prompt

Explain why increasing temperature increases the rate of a chemical reaction.

---

# AI Response

Increasing temperature increases the kinetic energy of molecules. As molecules move faster, collisions occur more frequently, and a greater proportion of collisions possess enough energy to overcome the activation energy barrier. As a result, the reaction rate increases.

# Improved Response

Increasing temperature increases the average kinetic energy of reacting molecules. According to collision theory, faster-moving molecules collide more frequently, and a greater proportion of these collisions have sufficient energy to overcome the activation energy barrier. These effective collisions lead to product formation, resulting in a higher reaction rate. For example, many reactions in the laboratory proceed noticeably faster when the reaction mixture is heated under controlled conditions.

---

# Lessons Learned

- AI responses should be scientifically accurate.
- Complete explanations are often better than brief answers.
- Including examples improves understanding.
- Structured evaluation helps identify opportunities for improvement.

---

# Final Recommendation

**Accept with Minor Improvements**

The response is factually correct and well written. Adding collision theory and a practical example would make it more comprehensive.
